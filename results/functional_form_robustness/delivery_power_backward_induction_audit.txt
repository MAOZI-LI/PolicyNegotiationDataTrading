Power delivery-time backward-induction audit
============================================
1. delivery_time_type="power" (reported as delivery_decay_type=power in CSV files) strictly uses t_pow=t1*(1+sum_i omega_i*(1-psr_i)^beta).
2. psr_i=(x_i+z_i)/|L_i| is clipped to [0,1] before applying the power term.
3. t0=1.0, t1=0.5 in the default calibrated market.
4. Stage 3 reuses provider best response because provider utility and risk cost do not directly depend on delivery time t; original discrete rounding/tie-breaking rules are preserved.
5. Stage 2 recomputes p_T^*(p_S) numerically because the log-time analytical TSP response is not valid under t_pow.
6. Stage 1 recomputes p_S^* using deterministic multi-resolution grid search over p_S>=0, not the old 120-point coarse grid.
7. Earlier values such as pS=5.042018 came from the first positive point of the old [1e-6,600] coarse grid and are not treated as final true equilibria.
8. No linear/log-time closed-form p_T^*(p_S) or p_S^* is used when delivery_time_type != log.
