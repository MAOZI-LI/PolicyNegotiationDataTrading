Piecewise delivery-time backward-induction audit
==============================================
1. delivery_time_type="piecewise" strictly uses the weighted overall psr=sum_i omega_i*psr_i.
2. The implemented normalized form is t/t1=1 if psr>=theta1; 1+c1*(theta1-psr) if theta2<=psr<theta1; and 1+c1*(theta1-theta2)+c2*(theta2-psr) if psr<theta2.
3. c1 and c2 are stored as scales relative to t1, so c1_scale=0.5 means c1=0.5*t1.
4. t0=1.0, t1=0.5 in the default calibrated market.
5. Stage 3 reuses provider best response because provider utility and risk cost do not directly depend on delivery time t; original discrete rounding/tie-breaking rules are preserved.
6. Stage 2 recomputes p_T^*(p_S) numerically because the log-time analytical TSP response is not valid under the piecewise delivery-time function.
7. Stage 1 recomputes p_S^* using deterministic multi-resolution grid search over p_S>=0.
8. No linear/log-time closed-form p_T^*(p_S) or p_S^* is used when delivery_time_type != log.
